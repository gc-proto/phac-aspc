Critical Mineral Exploration in Newfoundland and Labrador

Critical minerals are an essential part of many clean energy and technology products. They are important for building Canada's green and digital economy. That's why the government is supporting the mining industry in Newfoundland and Labrador to help it grow and meet the global demand for critical minerals. This support will help the province take advantage of opportunities in the critical minerals sector.

Investments to support exploration

The Government of Canada and the Government of Newfoundland and Labrador are investing a total of $10 million over the next three years to support two new initiatives focused on geoscience and exploration activities in the province. These initiatives will:

  • help identify potential areas for critical mineral exploration
  • provide increased support for junior exploration companies looking for critical minerals in Newfoundland and Labrador

These investments will help Newfoundland and Labrador capitalize on the growing demand for critical minerals.

What you can do

Newfoundland and Labrador has many critical minerals that are needed for clean energy and technology products. If you are interested in exploring for critical minerals in the province, you may be able to get support through the Junior Exploration Assistance Program. Contact the Government of Newfoundland and Labrador for more information.
