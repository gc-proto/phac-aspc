Eligibility

On this page

  • General Eligibility
  • Specific Situations

General Eligibility

  • You must be a resident of Canada
  • You do not need to be employed or have any other source of income to qualify for GMI

Specific Situations

There may be some specific situations in which you are not eligible for GMI. These include:

  • You are incarcerated in a prison or other correctional facility
  • You are not physically present in Canada

If you are unsure about your eligibility for GMI, you can contact the GMI program for more information and to have your specific situation assessed.
